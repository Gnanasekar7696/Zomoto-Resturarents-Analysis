Zomato Restaurant Analysis by Country
This project analyzes Zomato restaurant data across different countries to understand restaurant distribution, ratings, cuisines, and customer trends.
The analysis is performed using Excel, SQL, and Power BI to generate interactive dashboards and insights.

**Project Overview**

The goal of this project is to explore the Zomato dataset and answer key business questions such as:

Which countries have the most restaurants?

What are the most popular cuisines?

Which cities have the highest rated restaurants?

How do ratings vary across countries?

What is the distribution of restaurant price ranges?

The results are visualized in Power BI dashboards for easy interpretation.

** Dataset**

The dataset contains information about restaurants listed on Zomato, including:

Restaurant Name

Country

City

Cuisine Type

Average Cost for Two

Rating

Votes

Online Delivery Availability

Table Booking Availability




**Key Insights**

India has the highest number of restaurants listed on Zomato.

Most restaurants fall within the mid-price range.

North Indian, Chinese, and Fast Food are among the most popular cuisines.

Restaurants with online delivery and table booking tend to have higher customer engagement.

Certain cities show consistently higher average ratings.
